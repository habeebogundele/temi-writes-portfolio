What I Do

Services That Take Your
Book from Draft to Done

Every author deserves a publishing partner who understands both the craft of writing and the mechanics of publishing. Here is how I help you get there.

01
✍️
Book Editing
Developmental editing, line editing, copyediting, and proofreading. I work through every layer of your manuscript to ensure clarity, consistency, and compelling storytelling before your book reaches any reader.
02
📐
Book Formatting
Professional interior layout for print and digital formats. Your book will look typeset and polished across Kindle, ePub, and print-ready PDF, meeting every platform's exact specifications without compromise.
03
🚀
KDP Publishing Setup
End-to-end Amazon KDP publishing: metadata optimization, keyword research, category selection, and launch configuration. Your book goes live looking professional and positioned to be discovered from day one.
04
📖
Ghost Writing
Need your story told but not sure how to write it? I write in your voice, capturing your ideas and personality with authenticity. The book carries your name because it truly is your story, just beautifully written.
05
🎨
Book Description Writing
A compelling back cover description is the difference between a click and a scroll-past. I write keyword-rich, emotionally engaging blurbs that hook readers and convert browsers into buyers on Amazon and beyond.
06
📊
Manuscript Assessment
Not sure where your manuscript stands? I provide detailed, honest feedback on structure, pacing, voice, and market fit, giving you a clear roadmap of exactly what your book needs before you invest in full editing.
